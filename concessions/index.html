Open Concession Map

Each area on the map is the co-ordinates of an oil or gas "block", "field" or "concession" and provides basic information about contractors and available contracts for all 33 countries in Sub-Saharan Africa, for which oil blocks have been published.

The map represents the next evolution of the OpenOil concessions database and is designed as an open framework to contain further data, such as we published in our Open Data Tour for Tanzania, with which we hope to contribute to a formal, open data standard around extraction rights.
